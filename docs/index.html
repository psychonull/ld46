Objective

Avoid enemy projectiles while shooting and commanding yours

Controls

Player 1

  • Movement: A,S,D,W,
  • Turn projectile left/right: F,H
  • Projectile selection (previous/next): T,G
  • Shoot projectile: J

Player 2

  • Movement: Arrow Left,Arrow Down,Arrow Right,Arrow Up,
  • Turn projectile left/right: numpad 1,numpad 3
  • Projectile selection (previous/next): numpad 5,numpad 2
  • Shoot projectile: numpad 6

Gamepad support

Most twin stick gamepads should be supported, thought it's not widely tested

  • Movement: Left stick,
  • Turn projectile left/right: Right stick
  • Projectile selection (previous/next): LB,RB
  • Shoot projectile: RT
